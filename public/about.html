About Us

We're Building the Future of Student Portfolios

KePolio was born from a simple idea —” every student, from every stream, deserves a professional way to showcase their talent, without the complexity of traditional hosting.

Our Mission

We believe talent shouldn't be hidden behind any barriers. KePolio —” Know Everyone's Portfolio —” makes it effortless for students across all streams —” arts, commerce, science, engineering, and beyond —” to showcase their work and share a professional profile link with the world.

No complex setup. No barriers. Just add your work, build your profile, and share —” in under a minute.

Our Values

Simplicity First

We remove complexity so students can focus on what matters —” building great projects.

Accessible to All

Free for students. No hidden costs, no premium gates, no barriers to entry.

Security & Trust

Your profile and project data are protected with industry-standard security and reliability.

The Team

KePolio is built by a small team of developers and designers who are passionate about empowering the next generation of talented students across every discipline.
